Capture settings

The images are captured with normal lighting, auto-focused and fixed exposure settings (including aperture, shutter speed and ISO-value). The native resolution of each captured image is 4272x2848, which is used for the camera calibration with Bundler software (the calibration result is provided in each dataset). Then the images are resized by 15% to 641x427, for 3D reconstruction.
